About Our Dormitory System

Ensuring quality student accommodation with modern facilities

Why Choose WSU Dormitories?

Wolayita Sodo University provides state-of-the-art dormitory facilities designed to create a conducive learning environment for all students. Our dormitory management system ensures:

  • Safe and secure living environment
  • Modern facilities and amenities
  • Efficient room allocation system
  • 24/7 maintenance support
  • Proximity to academic buildings
  • Affordable accommodation rates

Our system manages all aspects of student accommodation including room assignments, fee payments, maintenance requests, and more.
